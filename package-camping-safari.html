12-Day Namibia Camping Safari

Fully immersive wilderness experience under the stars

12 Days Full Camping Adventure

Wilderness Immersion

Experience Namibia in its purest form with our full camping safari. This journey is designed for adventurous travelers who want to connect deeply with nature, sleeping under the stars in remote locations and experiencing the raw beauty of Namibia's wilderness.

Duration:

12 Days / 11 Nights

Group Size:

4-10 Travelers

Accommodation:

100% Camping

Wilderness Level:

High

What's Included

  • 11 nights camping at scenic locations
  • All meals prepared at camp
  • Selected beverages (water, soft drinks, coffee/tea)
  • All guided activities and game drives
  • National park and conservation fees
  • 4x4 safari vehicle with experienced guide
  • Full camping equipment (tents, sleeping bags, mats)
  • Camp kitchen with cooking equipment
  • Camp chairs and tables
  • Portable shower and toilet facilities
  • Cooler box for food storage

What's Not Included

  • International flights
  • Travel insurance
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Personal expenses
  • Optional activities
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Sleeping bag (available for hire)
  • Personal camping gear

Camping & Wilderness Highlights

  • Remote Campsites: Exclusive locations away from crowds
  • Stargazing: Some of the world's clearest night skies
  • Campfire Evenings: Stories under the African sky
  • Wild Cooking: Meals prepared over open fire
  • Nature Sounds: Fall asleep to wilderness sounds
  • Sunrise Moments: Wake up with the sun in pristine locations
  • Wildlife Proximity: Camp near wildlife areas (safely)

Camping Route & Destinations

Days 1-2 Windhoek • Equipment briefing • First camp setup
Days 3-4 Namib Desert • Sossusvlei camping • Dune exploration
Day 5 Swakopmund • Coastal camp • Optional activities
Days 6-7 Damaraland • Wilderness camping • Desert elephants
Days 8-10 Etosha National Park • Camping at park campsites
Day 11 Waterberg Plateau • Final wilderness camp
Day 12 Return to Windhoek • Equipment clean-up
