Sandwich Harbour Day Tour

Where the Namib Desert meets the Atlantic Ocean

Tour Overview

Experience one of Namibia's most spectacular natural wonders where towering sand dunes plunge directly into the Atlantic Ocean. This unique ecosystem is a paradise for photographers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Tour Highlights

  • 4x4 adventure through coastal dunes
  • Bird watching at Walvis Bay Lagoon (flamingos, pelicans)
  • Photography at dramatic desert-ocean interface
  • Fresh seafood lunch (optional add-on)
  • Professional guide with local knowledge

Itinerary

07:30 Pickup from your accommodation in Walvis Bay/Swakopmund
08:30 Bird watching at Walvis Bay Lagoon
10:00 4x4 drive through the dunes to Sandwich Harbour
12:00 Lunch at scenic spot (packed or seafood restaurant)
14:00 Exploration and photography session
16:00 Return to accommodation
