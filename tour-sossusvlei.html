Sossusvlei & Deadvlei Experience

Walk among 900-year-old trees in the world's tallest sand dunes

Tour Overview

Experience the iconic red dunes of Sossusvlei and the haunting beauty of Deadvlei. This tour takes you to the heart of the Namib Desert, where you'll witness some of Africa's most photographed landscapes.

Tour Highlights

  • Sunrise climb of Big Daddy or Dune 45
  • Photography at Deadvlei's ancient camel thorn trees
  • 4x4 drive through Sesriem Canyon
  • Stargazing in the desert (minimal light pollution)
  • Luxury desert lodge accommodation

2-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Morning: Depart Windhoek, scenic drive through Namib-Naukluft Park
Afternoon: Arrive at desert lodge, sunset dune walk
Evening: Dinner under the stars
Day 2 Pre-dawn: Depart for Sossusvlei to catch sunrise
Morning: Climb dunes, visit Deadvlei and Hiddenvlei
Afternoon: Sesriem Canyon exploration, return to lodge
