Etosha Wildlife Safari

Experience Africa's premier wildlife destination

Tour Overview

Explore Etosha National Park, one of Africa's largest and most accessible game reserves. With its vast salt pan and numerous waterholes, Etosha offers incredible wildlife viewing opportunities year-round.

Tour Highlights

  • Morning and afternoon game drives
  • Nighttime waterhole viewing at Okaukuejo
  • See the Big 5 (lion, leopard, elephant, rhino, buffalo)
  • Professional guide with wildlife expertise
  • Comfortable lodge accommodation inside the park

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Morning: Depart Windhoek, drive to Etosha
Afternoon: Arrive at lodge, afternoon game drive
Evening: Waterhole viewing at floodlit Okaukuejo
Day 2 Full Day: Game drives to different park sections
Evening: Sundowner drinks, night safari (optional)
Day 3 Morning: Final game drive, return to Windhoek

What's Included

Included:

  • All transportation in safari vehicle
  • 2 nights lodge accommodation
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Park entry fees and conservation levies
  • Professional guide/driver
  • Game drives as per itinerary

Not Included:

  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Optional night safari
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips for guide and lodge staff
