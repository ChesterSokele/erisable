14-Day Namibia All-Inclusive Experience

A well-balanced introduction to Namibia's highlights

Most Popular All-Inclusive 14 Days

Package Overview

Experience the best of Namibia in one complete journey. This package is ideal for travelers who want to see the country's highlights with all arrangements taken care of. Includes accommodation, daily meals, selected beverages, guided activities, park visits, and transport.

Duration:

14 Days / 13 Nights

Group Size:

2-8 Travelers

Accommodation:

Comfortable Lodges & Hotels

Transport:

4x4 Safari Vehicles

What's Included

  • 13 nights accommodation in lodges & hotels
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner daily)
  • Selected beverages (water, soft drinks)
  • All guided activities and excursions
  • National park entry fees
  • 4x4 transport with experienced driver
  • Support vehicle where required
  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Airport transfers

Not Included

  • International flights
  • Travel insurance
  • Premium beverages (alcohol)
  • Personal expenses & souvenirs
  • Optional activities
  • Tips and gratuities

Key Highlights

  • Namib Desert: World's oldest desert with surreal landscapes
  • Sossusvlei: Climb iconic red dunes, visit Deadvlei
  • Swakopmund: Coastal adventure activities
  • Etosha National Park: Premier wildlife viewing
  • Damaraland: Desert elephants & ancient rock art
  • Windhoek: Namibia's vibrant capital city

Sample Itinerary

Day 1-2 Arrive Windhoek • City tour • Acclimatization
Day 3-4 Namib Desert • Sossusvlei dunes • Deadvlei
Day 5-6 Swakopmund • Coastal activities • Marine wildlife
Day 7-9 Damaraland • Desert elephants • Rock formations
Day 10-12 Etosha National Park • Game drives • Waterhole viewing
Day 13-14 Return to Windhoek • Departure

Note: Itinerary can be customized based on your preferences.
