Skeleton Coast Expedition

Discover Namibia's remote and hauntingly beautiful coastline

Tour Overview

Journey to one of the world's last true wilderness areas - the Skeleton Coast. This remote and rugged coastline offers dramatic landscapes, historic shipwrecks, and unique desert-adapted wildlife found nowhere else on Earth.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore historic shipwrecks along the coast
  • Visit Cape Cross Seal Colony (over 100,000 seals)
  • See desert-adapted elephants and lions
  • Discover the Clay Castles geological formations
  • Experience remote wilderness camping

4-Day Expedition

Day 1 Morning: Depart Swakopmund, visit Cape Cross Seal Colony
Afternoon: Drive along coast, explore first shipwrecks
Day 2 Full Day: Explore remote beaches, search for desert wildlife
Day 3 Morning: Visit Clay Castles and Hoarusib Canyon
Afternoon: Look for desert-adapted elephants
Day 4 Morning: Final exploration, return to Swakopmund

What's Included

Included:

  • All transportation in expedition 4x4
  • 3 nights camping (fully equipped)
  • All meals prepared by camp chef
  • Park permits and entry fees
  • Professional expedition guide
  • Camping equipment (tents, sleeping bags, etc.)

Not Included:

  • Accommodation before/after tour
  • Personal travel insurance
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Optional scenic flight
