12-Day Namibia Lodge & Camping Adventure

Perfect blend of comfort and wilderness experience

12 Days Lodge & Camping Adventure

Package Overview

Experience the best of both worlds with our unique lodge and camping adventure. Enjoy comfortable lodge accommodations on some nights and authentic camping under the stars on others. This package offers a perfect balance between relaxation and wilderness immersion.

Duration:

12 Days / 11 Nights

Group Size:

4-8 Travelers

Accommodation:

50% Lodges, 50% Camping

Transport:

4x4 Safari Vehicle

What's Included

  • 6 nights lodge accommodation
  • 5 nights fully equipped camping
  • All meals throughout the journey
  • Selected beverages (water, soft drinks)
  • All guided activities and excursions
  • National park entry fees
  • 4x4 transport with experienced driver-guide
  • Camping equipment (tents, sleeping bags, mats)
  • Cooking equipment and camp chairs

What's Not Included

  • International flights
  • Travel insurance
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Personal expenses
  • Optional activities
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Sleeping bag (available for hire)

Key Highlights & Destinations

  • Sossusvlei Dunes: Climb the world's tallest sand dunes
  • Etosha National Park: Premier wildlife viewing at waterholes
  • Remote Wilderness Areas: Experience true off-grid camping
  • Coastal Landscapes: Atlantic coast and Sandwich Harbour
  • Damaraland: Desert-adapted elephants and ancient rock art
  • Swakopmund: Coastal town with adventure activities
  • Stargazing: Incredible night skies at remote campsites

Sample Itinerary

Days 1-2 Windhoek • City orientation • Lodge accommodation
Day 3 Namib Desert • First camping night • Sunset photography
Days 4-5 Sossusvlei • Dune climbing • Lodge stay
Day 6 Swakopmund • Coastal activities • Lodge accommodation
Days 7-8 Damaraland • Wilderness camping • Desert elephants
Days 9-10 Etosha National Park • Game drives • Lodge stay
Day 11 Waterberg Plateau • Final camping night
Day 12 Return to Windhoek • Departure

Note: Accommodation mix can be adjusted based on your preference.
