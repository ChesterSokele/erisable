Popular Self-Drive Routes

  • Classic Namibia Circuit: Windhoek • Sossusvlei • Swakopmund • Damaraland • Etosha • Waterberg (14-21 days)
  • Desert Explorer: Focus on Namib Desert, Sossusvlei, and coastal towns (10-14 days)
  • Wildlife Focus: Etosha National Park and surrounding reserves (7-10 days)
  • Remote Adventure: Skeleton Coast, Kaokoveld, and Kunene Region (14-21 days)
  • Family Friendly: Shorter distances, family accommodations, kid-friendly stops (10-12 days)

Self-Drive Tips

Fuel

Fill up whenever possible, stations can be 200km+ apart

Timing

Start early to avoid midday heat and for best wildlife viewing

Navigation

GPS is essential, but carry paper maps as backup
