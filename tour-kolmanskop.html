Kolmanskop Ghost Town

Photography experience in Namibia's abandoned diamond mining town

Tour Overview

Step back in time at Kolmanskop, a once-thriving diamond mining town now slowly being reclaimed by the desert. This tour is perfect for photographers and history enthusiasts looking to capture hauntingly beautiful images.

Tour Highlights

  • Early morning access for best photography light
  • Guided tour of abandoned buildings and homes
  • Learn about the diamond rush history
  • Photography tips from experienced guide
  • Visit to the museum and original mining equipment

Half-Day Itinerary

05:30 Pickup from Lüderitz accommodation (sunrise timing)
06:00 Arrive at Kolmanskop for golden hour photography
07:30 Guided walking tour of ghost town
09:00 Visit museum and historical exhibits
10:00 Free time for additional photography
11:00 Return to Lüderitz
