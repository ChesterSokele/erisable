Sample Flexible Itinerary

Your guide will work with you to create the perfect itinerary. Here's a suggested 10-day route:

Day 1 Windhoek • Meet your guide • City orientation
Days 2-3 Sossusvlei • Dune exploration • Photography sessions
Day 4 Swakopmund • Coastal drive • Optional activities
Days 5-6 Damaraland • Desert wildlife • Rock formations
Days 7-8 Etosha National Park • Game drives • Waterhole viewing
Day 9 Waterberg Plateau • Optional hiking • Scenic views
Day 10 Return to Windhoek • Departure

This itinerary can be shortened, extended, or completely customized.
